About MetaTask

MetaTask is a comprehensive service application designed to streamline workflows for organizations, small teams, companies, and individuals. Our platform integrates multiple services including workflow management and job planning functionality.

Our Mission

To provide organizations with a unified platform that simplifies complex business processes while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and data protection.

Key Features

  • GDPR compliant by design
  • Multi-service integration
  • Role-based access control
  • Scalable architecture
  • Open-source foundation

Technical Foundation

Built with Django, PostgreSQL, Redis, and modern web technologies, MetaTask provides a robust and secure platform for your organization's needs.
